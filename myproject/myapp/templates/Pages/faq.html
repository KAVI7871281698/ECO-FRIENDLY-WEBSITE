Knowledge Base

Conscious shopping, simplified.

At Ecomart, transparency is the root of our philosophy. We believe that informed choices are the most powerful tool for planetary change. Here is everything you need to know about our ecosystem.

🌿 Products & Sustainability
What defines the "Ecomart Standard" for eco-friendly products?
+

Every product hosted on Ecomart must undergo a rigorous 3-step verification process known as our "Green Lifecycle Audit." First, we evaluate the Source Material: we only accept biodegradable, recycled, or rapidly renewable resources. Second, the Production Ethics: our partners must provide proof of fair wages and low-waste manufacturing cycles.

Lastly, we look at the End-of-Life: can this product return to the earth without leaving a trace? By adhering to these strict pillars, we ensure that your purchase isn't just "less bad" for the environment, but actually part of a regenerative cycle that supports the planet.

Are your skincare and home products safe for sensitive skin?
+

Transparency is key here. Our Botanical Serum and Home Care lines are formulated without sulfates, parabens, synthetic fragrances, or petroleum-based ingredients. We prioritize "Whole Plant" science, using pure essential oils and cold-pressed botanical extracts.

However, because natural ingredients are potent, we highly recommend reviewing the full ingredient list provided on each product page. Our Verdana Smart Assistant can also provide a detailed analysis of any ingredient if you ask! When in doubt, always perform a small patch test before full application.

📦 Shipping & Logistics
How do you manage carbon-neutral shipping?
+

Most e-commerce operations prioritize speed at the cost of the environment. At Verdana, we take a different path. We utilize Consolidated Routing, which means our delivery vehicles operate on optimized paths that significantly reduce fuel consumption per package. Our packaging is 100% plastic-free, using cornstarch pellets and mushroom-based padding that you can compost at home.

For every kilometer your package travels, we invest in verified reforestation projects in Tamil Nadu and across India. This ensures that the carbon footprint of your delivery is not only neutralized but historically offset by the oxygen-producing trees we plant on your behalf.

What is your return policy for open products?
+

To reduce waste and carbon costs associated with return shipping, we encourage mindful purchasing. If a product arrives damaged or incorrect, we offer a 100% replacement or refund within 7 days of delivery. For hygiene reasons, we cannot accept returns for opened personal care or skincare products unless there is a quality defect.

If you find that a product doesn't suit your needs, we encourage you to "re-gift" it to a friend or family member—extending the product's life is the most sustainable option! If you truly need a return, please contact our support team to discuss the most eco-friendly way to process it.

🌳 Impact & Community
How does the Ecomart "One Tree" pledge work?
+

Our partnership with local reforestation NGOs is the heartbeat of Verdana. For every single order placed on our platform, we fund the planting of a native sapling. These aren't just any trees; we select species that support local biodiversity and help restore groundwater levels.

Once you register an account, your first tree is planted immediately. You can track this in your Personal Impact Dashboard, where we provide updates on the growth of the forests you are helping to build. This is our way of ensuring that every transaction leaves the world a little greener than we found it.
